Welcome To Team 13 Project

Here, our team is creating a website about Superstore, a leading company in the United States that sells various product categories such as technology, office supplies, and furniture.

About Superstore Dataset

This dataset provides detailed insights into the operations of a Superstore Giant, covering orders, customers, products, and sales. It includes essential order details like order IDs, dates, and shipping information, along with customer demographics and geographic data. Product information encompasses categories, sub-categories, and sales figures, while sales and profit data shed light on performance metrics such as discounts and profitability. With these insights, businesses can analyze sales performance, identify top-selling products, understand customer preferences, and optimize strategies to improve competitiveness in the market.
